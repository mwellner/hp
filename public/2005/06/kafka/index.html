Kafka

16.06.2005 in  kafka, lyrics, poem, time

Waiting.

Waiting for Kafka.

Waiting for Godot.

Writing.

Writing to pass the time.

Writing about Kafka.

20 minutes to pass.

20 lines with 20 letters.

Maybe more. Maybe less.

I am waiting.

Kafka is waiting.

Waiting.

Just waiting.

Spending time.

People pass.

Patience.

I have time.

Although time is nothing to have or not have.

You don’t have time.

You spend time.

But spending also implies possession.

Time spends you.

Time passes.

Time doesn’t care for you at all.

Time.
