workrave

Mon Dec 11, 2006 by Mathias Wellner in  tagebuch

Do you work most of your time at the computer? Then Workrave may be a good choice for you. It is designed to enforce smaller and larger breaks while you are working on your computer. The goal is to prevent repetitive strain injury (see Wikipedia entry), which can result from too much work in the same position. It also helps to give your eyes a rest from time to time. The program is open source and available for the platforms Linux and Windows.
