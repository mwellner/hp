ICORR 2007

Thu Jun 14, 2007 by Mathias Wellner in  tagebuch

It’s day 2 of the conference now, my interactive presentation was already on the first day. Some people were really interested and so this time passed away quickly. The presentations are – as to be expected at a conference of such dimensions – diverse in their contents and presentation level.

Conference venue: the palace hotel in Nordwijk, The Netherlands

The highlight so far was a trip to Delft University yesterday. We had a lab visit there and saw some very interesting robotic projects. Some images for your entertainment…

Dutch is a funny language, but most things sound familiar.

A haptic manipulator, which is commercially available. In the background you can see a donut-like shape that you can push around with a small sphere.
