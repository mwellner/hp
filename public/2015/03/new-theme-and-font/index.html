New Theme and Font

19.03.2015 in  www  cryout, css, Font, less, nirvana, style, theme, Wordpress

I have changed the appearance of my web site, giving the Nirvana WordPress theme a chance. My goal was to get away from Parabola, since we use that on dramateure.ch as well. The elements are roughly the same, but the look is slightly different.

One thing I noted with great delight – you can use the Less pre-processor when typing in your custom CSS. What I actually changed were table styles, they had defined quite large paddings, making my acting overview page look strange.

What I also changed was the default font. After hearing Erik Spiekermann on UX Munich, I am now giving Fira Sans a try. It was developed to look good on screen and hand-held devices.
