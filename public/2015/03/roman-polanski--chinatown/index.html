Roman Polanski – Chinatown

Sun Mar 15, 2015 in  arts  Chinatown, Faye Dunaway, Film Noir, Jack Nicholson, Roman Polanski

Chinatown is widely regarded as classic and surely one of Polanskis master pieces. It follows the film noir tradition, set in a drying and sun-flooded Los Angeles. The main character (the young Jack Nicholson), a private detective, is investigating the murder of the city’s chief water engineer. He is drawn in a jungle of corruption.
