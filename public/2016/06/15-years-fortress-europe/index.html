15 Years Fortress Europe

11.06.2016 in  politics  casualties, deaths, europe, fortress, map, refugees

The interactive map 15 Years Fortress Europe shows the more than 32’000 refugee deaths trying to enter Europe or stay in it. It is the sad result of many factors, ongoing wars and economic regression in many countries, overloaded boats, lack of perspectives and shutting down the relatively safe Balkan route.
