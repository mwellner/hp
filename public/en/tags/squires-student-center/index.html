Student Initiatives

20.09.2002 in  diary  blacksburg, virginia tech, squires student center

On Friday, most of the students organizations presented themselves in Squires Student Center. Interested to get involved in something I entered the room. It was packed with people, you could hear music playing from one corner, and a babble of voices was in the air. The organizations were organized in long rows of tables, with every organization only having two meters to present their material. So I went through, drowned in the many possibilities and smiles.
